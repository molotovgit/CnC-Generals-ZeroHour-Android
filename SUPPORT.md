# Getting help

Thanks for trying the port! Here's the fastest way to get unstuck.

## First, check the docs

- **Setting up your own game files** (step by step): [docs/SETUP-GAME-FILES.md](docs/SETUP-GAME-FILES.md)
- **Building the engine + app**: [BUILDING.md](BUILDING.md)
- **How things work / what was fixed**: [docs/PORTING-NOTES.md](docs/PORTING-NOTES.md) and
  [docs/fixes/](docs/fixes/)
- **Touch controls**: [docs/CONTROLS.md](docs/CONTROLS.md)
- **Device compatibility**: [docs/DEVICE-SUPPORT.md](docs/DEVICE-SUPPORT.md)

## Where to ask

| You want to… | Go to |
|---|---|
| Ask a question, share a build, discuss ideas | **[Discussions](https://github.com/molotovgit/CnC-Generals-ZeroHour-Android/discussions)** |
| Report a crash or wrong behavior | **[Issues → Bug report](https://github.com/molotovgit/CnC-Generals-ZeroHour-Android/issues/new/choose)** |
| Suggest a feature or a device to support | **[Issues → Feature request](https://github.com/molotovgit/CnC-Generals-ZeroHour-Android/issues/new/choose)** |
| Report a security problem | **[Security policy](SECURITY.md)** (private reporting) |

When reporting a bug, please include your **device model, SoC/GPU, Android version**, and the
app's private log — see [CONTRIBUTING.md](CONTRIBUTING.md).

## What we can't help with

- Providing, finding, or linking **game data**. You must supply your own retail files — see
  [docs/GAME-FILES.md](docs/GAME-FILES.md). Piracy questions will be closed.
- Support for cracked or pirated copies of the game.
